Here we will learn a bit more about dogs.

What do I feed my dog?

You can feed your dog:

  • Dog food from the grocery store
  • Chicken you cooked at home(without spices!)
  • Rice you (also) cooked at home
  • An alternative meat which you also cooked at home
  • Trash that your dog was able to eat because you forgot to close the lid to the trash can
  • Grass or other fauna while at the park
  • The food of other dogs who were naive to leave it uneaten
  • Alot of things, because dogs will eat anything

You can NOT feed your dog:

  • Raisins (not sure why, but they can't)
  • Chocolate
  • Spackle (dogs LOVE spackle; I experienced it first hand)
  • Tennis balls
  • Electronic devices
  • Candy
  • Your children

And finally, here are some things to do with your dog:

  • Play fetch
  • Cuddle
  • Watch TV
  • Go to the lake or ocean
  • Chase squirrels outside
  • Go on a walk
  • Go on a run
  • Go on a sit (outside)
  • Have a playdate with other dogs
  • Go to the dog park
